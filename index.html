THERE WHEN YOU NEED US:

As everyday life becomes more challenging, a little help can go a long way. For over 5 years, The Liberty home care has been providing one-on-one care and companionship to those in need of a helping hand. From a few hours to the whole day, we're there when and where you need us to help make life easier and maximize independence! The Liberty home care, caregivers are employees, not subcontractors, who are thoroughly screened and insured by workers' compensation and unemployment to ensure you and your loved onesare fully protected. Our Compassionate Caregivers are extensively trained, and many have experience with speciality care, including Alzheimer's, Dementia, Parkinson's and Hospice care.
